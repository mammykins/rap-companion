Chapter 4 Exemplar RAP {exemplar}

In this Chapter we demonstrate a RAP package developed in collaboration with the Department for Culture Media and Sport .

In this exemplar project we aimed at a high level of automation to demonstrate what is possible, and because DCMS have a skilled data scientist on hand to maintain and develop the project. Nonetheless, in the course of this work, statisticians at DCMS have undertaken training in R, and we have spent time to ensure that the software development practices such as version control, unit testing, and continuous integration are embedded within the team that owns the publication.
